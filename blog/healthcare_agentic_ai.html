AI-Powered Healthcare Assistant for PM-JAY

Make Public Healthcare Transparent, Responsive, and Patient-Centric

May 21, 2025
AI Solutions, Healthcare
8 min read

What if every PM-JAY beneficiary could talk to a digital assistant—one that instantly confirms their entitlements, finds nearby hospitals, checks real-time bed availability, and translates complex policy into human language?

This isn't the future. It's happening now.
And Pragyametrics is deploying it to transform public healthcare delivery in India.

Why Healthcare Access Needs a Rethink

India's Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) scheme is the world's largest government-funded health insurance program—offering ₹5 lakh annual coverage to over 100 million vulnerable families. But despite its scale, a silent barrier persists:

Common Questions Without Answers

"Am I eligible?"

"Is cancer treatment covered?"

"Where can I get surgery under this scheme?"

"Are ICU beds available in my city right now?"

Without a responsive system, these questions often go unanswered—causing underutilization, misinformation, and frustration at the front lines of care.

The Critical Question

In a world where AI predicts protein folding, translates speech in real time, and flies drones autonomously—why can't it answer a mother in Satara asking, "Can my son get heart surgery under PM-JAY?"

The answer?
Until now, no system connected India's complex policy ecosystem with real, patient-facing intelligence.

We fixed that.

What Is the PM-JAY AI Assistant?

It's more than a chatbot.
It's not just a helpdesk or a website.

It's a fully integrated LLM-powered digital assistant that understands user queries, retrieves data from PM-JAY policy packages, hospital databases, and even real-time bed availability—then returns clear, personalized answers instantly.

Built using:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) for natural dialogue
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ground answers in real data
  • A searchable vector index of 1900+ treatment packages
  • Real-time hospital and bed status from our API pipeline

This assistant doesn't just give information.
It guides beneficiaries through the healthcare maze—in their language, with their needs, using real-world data.

From Automation to Patient-Level Intelligence

Where basic portals end, AI-guided assistance begins.

Search engines show thousands of pages.
Our system tells you:

"Yes, chemotherapy is covered under PM-JAY. You are entitled to up to ₹1.5 lakh per year for this treatment. Nearest empaneled centers are: Asha Cancer Institute and Fortis Mulund."

Real Prompts. Real Responses. Real Impact.

The Technology Behind the System

Layer Stack
Frontend ReactJS (mobile-first, multilingual UI planned)
Backend Services Google Cloud Kubernetes Engine (GKE)
LLM Inference GCP-hosted OpenAI / LLaMA models
Semantic Search Pinecone (vector database)
Hospital Data Astra DB (with GraphQL access)
Real-Time Sync GCP Cloud Scheduler + cross-cloud batch pipeline

Our hybrid-cloud architecture ensures speed, resilience, and scale, with secure role-based access and API-first extensibility.
